Net Worth Calculator

Enter your assets and liabilities to see your net worth. Data is saved locally in your browser.

How it works
Net Worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities.
  • Assets: things you own with positive value — cash, savings, investments, retirement accounts, real estate equity, vehicles.
  • Liabilities: money you owe — credit-card balances, mortgage, student/auto loans, lines of credit.
  • Click + Add Asset / Add Liability to add custom rows. Use Save to persist entries to this browser; Reset clears them.
  • All data stays on your device — nothing is sent anywhere.
